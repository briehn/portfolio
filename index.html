Track My Apps

AI-powered job application tracker for saving roles, tracking statuses, managing notes, and analyzing job descriptions with structured AI output.

Built and deployed a full-stack job application tracker with Google OAuth, user-owned job/note data, dashboard summaries, archived jobs, AI job description analysis, AI profile extraction, transient profile-to-job matching, CSV import/export, and production AI usage limits.

Implemented features include Google OAuth, job creation, listing, detail editing, status tracking, archived jobs, archive/delete, notes, responsive UI, and Vitest coverage.

Current focus: maintaining the shipped AI and data workflows while keeping the tracker fast and reliable.
